More about me

Nancy Salazar is a passionate and driven IT engineer with a deep commitment to fostering digital empowerment through technology 🚀💻. She is the founder of tekis.services, a startup that offers digital skills development and technology implementation for SMEs 🏢🔧. With a background in Information Technology from the Universidad Tecnológica de León and currently pursuing a master's degree in IT management at the Tec de Monterrey 🎓, Nancy has a strong foundation in both technical expertise and business acumen 🤓📊. Her work reflects a dedication to bridging the gender gap in tech, especially through initiatives like Tecnolochicas 👩‍💻✨, where she mentors and teaches web programming to young women.


In addition to her entrepreneurial endeavors, Nancy is a recognized educator and mentor in various prestigious institutions 📚👩‍🏫. She teaches Technical Writing and Code Documentation at Platzi and Creative Coding: Data Visualization in Python at CENTRO university 🖥️📈. Nancy also serves as a mentor in entrepreneurship, technology, and innovation for leading platforms like Google for Startups, Startup México, and Techstars 🚀💡. Her efforts to promote technology inclusion extend to her role as a speaker and thought leader 🎤🌟, where she has delivered talks at international conferences, including TEDx, and has been recognized as a Top Voice in Technology and Innovation by LinkedIn in 2022 🏆💬.


Nancy’s influence stretches across several sectors 🌍. She is a counselor in technology for the International Film Festival of Alternative Media 🎬🎥 and a partner at Women in Cinema and Television Mexico 🎞️💪. Her contributions to the tech industry have earned her recognition among Mexico’s top 100 inspiring entrepreneurs 💯✨ and as one of the 50 women building Web3 in Latin America 🌐👩‍💻. Known for her collaboration with major companies such as IBM, Google, and Amazon 🖥️🔗, Nancy continues to inspire and lead within the tech community, always championing the role of women in STEM 👩‍🔬🌈.
